If I were a senior SDE with ~10 years of experience building an industry-level HRMS (without a specific industry), I’d design it to be **modular, scalable, and configurable**. Here’s what I would include:

### 🔹 Core HR Features

* [x] Employee management (profiles, documents, lifecycle)
* [x] Organizational structure (departments, roles, hierarchy)
* [ ] Onboarding & offboarding workflows
* [x] Employee self-service portal

### 🔹 Recruitment & Hiring

* [ ] Job posting & applicant tracking (ATS)
* [ ] Resume parsing & candidate pipeline
* [ ] Interview scheduling & feedback system
* [ ] Offer management

### 🔹 Attendance & Leave

* [x] Time tracking (biometric/API integrations)
* [x] Leave management (multiple policies)
* [ ] Shift scheduling & roster management
* [x] Holiday calendar

### 🔹 Payroll & Compensation

* [x] Salary structure configuration
* [x] Automated payroll processing
* [x] Tax calculations (country configurable)
* [x] Payslips & reimbursement management

### 🔹 Performance Management

* [ ] Goal setting (OKRs/KPIs)
* [ ] Performance reviews (360° feedback)
* [ ] Appraisals & increments tracking

### 🔹 Learning & Development

* [ ] Training programs & course tracking
* [ ] Skill matrix & certifications
* [ ] Learning recommendations

### 🔹 Compliance & Policies

* [ ] Policy management & acknowledgments
* [ ] Legal compliance (region configurable)
* [x] Audit logs & reporting

### 🔹 Analytics & Reporting

* [x] HR dashboards (attrition, hiring, payroll insights)
* [ ] Custom report builder
* [x] Export (Excel, PDF)

### 🔹 Communication & Collaboration

* [ ] Internal announcements
* [ ] Chat or integrations (Slack/Teams)
* [x] Notifications (email/SMS/in-app)

### 🔹 Security & Access Control

* [x] Role-based access control (RBAC)
* [x] Data encryption & privacy controls
* [ ] Multi-factor authentication (MFA)

### 🔹 Integrations

* [x] Third-party APIs (accounting, biometrics, job portals)
* [ ] Single Sign-On (SSO)
* [x] REST APIs for extensibility

### 🔹 Tech & Scalability Considerations

* [x] Microservices architecture (if large scale)
* [x] Cloud-ready (AWS/Azure/GCP)
* [x] Multi-tenant support (for SaaS)
* [x] High availability & backup strategy

### 🔹 Optional Advanced Features

* [ ] AI-based resume screening
* [ ] Chatbot for HR queries
* [ ] Predictive analytics (attrition risk)
* [ ] Mobile app support

This approach keeps the system **generic enough for any industry** while allowing **customization per client needs**.
